Eye-tracking data columns:

TA = Duration of fixation to Target Animal during the time window

TI  = Duration of fixation to Target Instrument during the time window

CA = Duration of fixation to Competitor Animal during the time window

CI = Duration of fixation to Competitor Instrument during the time window

subj = subject number

trialID = item number

order = trial order

cond = Verb bias Condition:
0 - Instrument-biased verb
1 - Modifier-biased verb
2 - Equi-biased verb
9 - filler
